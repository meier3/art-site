DANICA MEIER

Artist Statement

The idea behind this video involves a number of elements. Overall, I tried to play around with various old TV clips to highlight some of the more negative qualities of television media. The media we see on television screens, especially during the time when some of these clips were airing, very often promotes messages of cartoon violence, exaggerated consumerism, and patriarchal misogyny, which are the three main themes which appear throughout this film. Some of the more subtle motifs have to do with the short attention span which arises from channel-hopping and the mindless repetition of actions, sequences, and storylines. The goal of this video was not to criticize television as a source of entertainment and information, but rather to point out some of the negative effects it can have on society.
